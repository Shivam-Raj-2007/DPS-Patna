CLASS XII FAREWELL


On the 10th of February, 2023, Delhi Public School, Patna hosted a farewell ceremony for the Batch of 2023. This marked the end of their school life and the beginning of a new chapter in their lives. The seniors entered the campus to the lively beat of drums, and the junior wing teachers greeted them by adorning their heads with a tilak. The theme of the event was graduation, reminiscing the farewell they received in Prep with similar graduation hats, which were prepared by the 11th graders. As stated by the Headmistress, “The only difference is, in Prep they were beginning their school life, and today they’re ready to take on the world”. The campus was adorned with vibrant banners and a breathtaking hanging flex. Colorful Chinese fans crowned the entrance of each classroom.

The students then proceeded to their respective classrooms to collect the Takshila mementoes. The school presented them with a collection of souvenirs, including a copy of "A History of India Through 75 Objects", a book which details the history of the nation through its significant objects. Accompanying the book was an inspiring letter from the Pro-Vice Chairman, printed on eco-friendly plantable paper embedded with seeds. As a symbol of their time at the school, the students were also given a memento featuring the class picture and a capsule of soil from the school grounds, serving as a lasting reminder of their memories and experiences. Post the distribution, it was time for the main event; the assembly.The assembly began with a warm welcome from the choir, who sang soulful shlokas and prayers. The comperes introduced the event and invited the dance team to present their Ganesh Vandana, symbolizing the juniors’ seeking blessings from the almighty for their seniors. The Headmistress delivered an emotional final address, followed by speeches from Chetna ma’am, Madhavi ma’am, and Sanjay sir.

Shorya Akhil and Kushagra Verma then presented a self-composed poem, expressing their admiration and memories of their seniors. The choir then performed a musical presentation, encapsulating the 14-year journey of the batch. The Principal addressed the batch for the final time, invoking reverence and reflection among the students. Each class then had a representative, who spoke about their experiences and reflections. The Vice Head-boy and Head-girl also expressed their gratitude and promised to uphold the legacy of their seniors. The event concluded with speeches from the Head Boy and Head Girl, who beautifully summarized their 14-year journey and attributed their success to those who have been a part of it. The farewell ceremony ended with the distribution of food packets, marking the end of a memorable day and an era. Adios, Batch of 2023!
