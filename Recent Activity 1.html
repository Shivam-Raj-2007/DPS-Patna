ROBOFEST


On 18 and 19 August 2022, Delhi Public School, Patna hosted the Robofest-2022 for the young minds eager to learn and absorb knowledge in the field of technology. The Robofest-2022 comprises of two events happening side by side for the budding technocrats. The first event was an interschool event for students of Classes VI to VIII. This 2-day event was related to Robotics and was conducted in the Multipurpose Hall of the school. The second event was an intra-school event for students of Classes IX to XII, named AI Boot Camp, wherein the participating students learned about Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning. The Artificial Intelligence Boot Camp was conducted in the ATL Lab of the school. Robocon, the two-day event was put up with the help of. Mr. Varun Anand, Dr. Sarvjeet Herald and Ms. Zeeba Ziya, our guests from the Robogenius crew who helped in training the participants about robot construction using Lego Mindstorms kit. There were ten schools from Patna which took part in this fest.

The day began with the inauguration ceremony of the fest. The expert from the Robogenius team, Mr. Varun Anand carried further the proceedings of the day. Day 1 was centred on training in LEGO Mindstorms NXT kit, understanding the basic idea of building robots and block programming concepts. A workshop on Artificial Intelligence Bootcamp was arranged by RoboGenius for the students of classes IX-XII at Delhi Public School Patna.The resource person for the workshop was Dr. Sarvjit Herald. Dr. Herald elaborated on the accuracy and the effectiveness of the same. He made himself clear by using relevant examples like Amazon Echo etc. and unfolding the working of the same. The students were given a broader view by giving real-life examples of AI applications. Further elaborating on the types of data and accuracy of the data, Dr. Herald also helped the participants to choose the correct career option suitable according to their interests by showing them the opportunities in the IT sector.

Then an exercise was given to the students to have a better understanding on data sets and it was followed by a brief explanation of problem solving.. Finally, students were given the concluding assignment, which was an interesting problem to solve by creating an Artificial Intelligence using "Decision Tree Classifier" a type of Supervised Learning Algorithm using scikit-learn and Pandas library in python. Children learned how to test their model and predict accuracy.The students who completed the challenge, were asked to present their project individually on the projector and explain their code. This marked the end of the workshop and the results of first five winners was announced for award ceremony.In the closing ceremony, the wining students were awarded prizes and two-day event came to a halt, only to witness the same next year.
