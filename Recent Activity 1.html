THINGS AROUND US


Major co-curricular activity ‘Things Around Us’ was conducted for students of class Nursery to III on January 28, 2022. Plethora of activities were planned to engage the children in fun filled and creative ways. The workstation of teachers were decorated and teachers were dressed up as seasonal flowers and welcomed children on the melodious song composed by music department on the theme “Things around us. In class Nursery & Prep, the day started with the Attendance activity, where the children responded to their names by speaking what they see around themselves like, “I see…”. There after a video was shown to the children which showed various things, We could see around us.

Such as an Aeroplan, buildings, road signals, hospitals, garden, school and many other things. Children enjoyed the video. The most entertaining and thrilling activity for class nursery students were colouring the Bear on a white paper and marking the sense organs. Class Prep students were thrilled to do the “long necked giraffe” in which they made a paper giraffe and later they named it. The last activity planned for the day was the paper bouquet where teacher demonstrated how to make it using origami method. The last activity planned for the day was “Explore your surroundings “where children brought the things while explaining their uses in terms of smell, taste, feel or hear. The day concluded on a very positive note making this virtual event a memorable one for our little ones. Class I children were shown a story on the importance of taking care and keeping the surroundings clean which was followed by craft activity ‘My Cute animals’ made by using the ice cream sticks and decorate it as pretty as picture. The most entertaining and thrilling activity that made the kids very enthusiastic, was the “Balancing book Dance” in which kids placed a book over their head and danced the music is played. Class II children drew 10 things which they see around them in their scrap file and coloured them. In the Language Activity , children described the things they had drawn during the activity.

The next activity was “Caring for Creation” where children were sensitized towards their environment and encouraged to think of ways of taking care of it. Class III students showed their excitement by giving various answers and examples. Followed with this interesting discussion, lot of activities were conducted in different groups like ‘What do I see Around?’ In this activity children drew ten living and non - living things, ‘Language Activity’, where children described the things, they had drawn during the activity. The day concluded on a very positive note making this virtual event a memorable one for our little ones. They learned that everything around has a purpose.
