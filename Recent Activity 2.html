INDEPENDENCE DAY CELEBRATION


The 75th Independence Day was celebrated with all its solemnity and grandeur at Delhi Public School Patna. The event began with the hoisting of the National Flag by the Principal. This was followed by the singing of the National Anthem. The National Anthem was followed by taking the pledge by our Principal, all staff members and the students. Later, Principal addressed the gathering and spoke about the significance of Independence Day and exhorted the students to inculcate, develop and spread the ideals and sentiments of patriotism.

To commemorate the Freedom struggle of our heroes of India’s Independence, the comperes- Ryan, Agrima and Yashaswi invited the participants with their remarkable words. It began with poetry recitation by Ridhima Raj which was followed by the speech given by Ankit Raj. These words were enough to generate the spark of patriotism among the students. Students of the Theatre club represented the ideologies of the freedom fighters Bhagat singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev in the play ‘Shaheed’.

The spirit of nationalism is important for understanding the true value of Independence and patriotic songs for children are the best means to do so. Such songs encourage us to do well for our country and bring a sense of responsibility towards the nation. The Choir Group touched our hearts by euphonious patriotic song ‘kranti ki mashal se’. The exuberant performance by the dynamic Services staff filled the atmosphere with allegiance. The mega event of Independence Day concluded with exuberant dance performances by students. Everyone enjoyed the celebration of this day with great zeal and enthusiasm.
